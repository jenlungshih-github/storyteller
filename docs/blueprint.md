# **App Name**: Story Teller

## Core Features:

- Interactive Template: Provides a fill-in-the-blanks template to guide users through the story creation process.
- Prompt Generation: Generates story prompts based on user input, such as character type, setting, and desired lesson, offering inspiration for new stories. The prompts may suggest surprising story twists.
- Story Expander: Expands the user's outline into a fully written childrenâ€™s story. Using a large language model, this tool suggests creative ideas for character, adventure, and story resolution.
- Feedback Submission: Allows users to submit their story outlines and drafts for feedback from the application. No AI processing will happen during feedback submission.
- Example Library: Maintained list of curated simple story examples for users to review.
- Skill Practice: Displays instructions on particular writing skills. Skills will focus on concise language, adding sounds, unexpected surprises, rhythm and emotion.
- Multilingual Support: In Chinese and English

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: A soft sky blue (#87CEEB) to evoke a sense of calmness and imagination, mirroring a clear, bright day conducive to storytelling.
- Background color: Very light desaturated blue (#E0F7FA) provides a gentle, non-distracting backdrop that enhances readability.
- Accent color: A gentle, contrasting yellow (#FFECB3) for highlighting important elements, inspired by the warmth of the sun in a child's drawing.
- Headline font: 'Belleza', a humanist sans-serif for a playful, design-oriented look.
- Body font: 'Alegreya', a humanist serif for its elegance, intellectual feel, and excellent readability.
- Use simple, whimsical icons to represent different story elements and actions.
- Maintain a clean, uncluttered layout to make it easy for writers to navigate and focus on content.
- Use subtle animations to create a sense of playfulness.
